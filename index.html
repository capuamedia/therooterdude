When you have a clogged drain emergency, call The Rooter Dude for prompt service throughout Middle Tennessee. We use state-of-the-art video camera equipment to show you exactly what's going on, plus hydro jetting to clear tough clogs, descale pipes, and remove grease buildup.

High-Quality Water Line Replacement

Water lines become vulnerable to ground movement, water pressure, mineral buildup, and freezing as they age. Our licensed, bonded, and insured technicians use specialized methods to access the main water line without digging up your yard, landscaping, or driveway.
